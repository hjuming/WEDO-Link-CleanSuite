CleanSuite Tutorial

Learn how to optimize your macOS system easily

1. Install CleanSuite

Download the .pkg installer from the official website and follow the installation steps. Once installed, you will find CleanSuite in your Applications folder.

2. Main Interface Overview

  • Quick Clean: Remove cache, logs, and temp files.
  • Deep Clean: More thorough cleanup for heavy users.
  • App Cleaning: Clear LINE, Mail, Safari/Chrome data.
  • Scheduler: Automate daily/weekly/monthly cleanups.
  • Settings: Customize preferences & cleanup levels.

3. Cleaning Modes

Quick Clean: Recommended daily or during work days.

Deep Clean: Recommended weekly or monthly.

App Cleaning: Use when LINE or Mail consumes too much space.

4. Scheduler Setup

Open CleanSuite → Go to Scheduler → Choose frequency (daily/weekly/monthly). macOS will automatically run cleanup tasks in the background.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why is the freed space small?
    It may indicate your system is already clean. Try Deep Clean.
  • Q: LINE/Mail cleanup incomplete?
    Please fully quit the app and try again.
  • Q: Scheduler not working?
    Ensure macOS background permissions are allowed and the Mac is not fully shut down.
