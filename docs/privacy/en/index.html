Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 27, 2023

Thank you for using CleanSuite ("the Software"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

The Software does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) during its operation. All cleaning operations (such as cache clearing, mail cleaning, etc.) are performed locally on your device, and no files or data are uploaded to our servers.

2. Data Usage

We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., crash reports, feature usage frequency) to help us improve software performance and user experience. This data does not contain any personal identity information.

3. Third-Party Services

The official website of the Software may use third-party analysis tools such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel to understand website traffic and user behavior. These tools may use Cookies to collect anonymous information.

4. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
